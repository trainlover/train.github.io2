How much do you know about our trains?


There are two rail operators in Singapore:

  1. Singapore Mass Rapid Transit (SMRT)

    • North South Line

      • From Jurong East to Marina South Pier

      Train models:
      • C151 Kawasaki Heavy Industries
      • C151A Kawasaki Heavy Industries
      • C651 Siemens
      • C751B Kawasaki Heavy Industries & Nippon Sharyo

    • East West Line

      • From Joo Koon to Pasir Ris

      Train models:
      • C151
      • C151A
      • C651
      • C751B

    • Circle Line

      • From Harbourfront to Dhoby Ghaut

      Train models:
      • C830 Alstom Metropolis
      • C830A Alstom Metropolis

  2. Singapore Bus Services (SBS)

    • North East Line

      • From Harbourfront to Punggol

      • C751 Alstom Metropolis
      • C751A Alstom Metropolis

    • Downtown Line

      • From Bukit Panjang to Expo

      Train models:
      • C951 Bombardier Transportation
